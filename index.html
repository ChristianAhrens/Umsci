Overview

Umsci is a small utility to connect to a d&b Soundscape signal engine via OCA/OCP.1 to control soundobject positions in accordance to standard immersive output formats. The app subscribes to soundobject and loudspeaker position values and displays them on a 2D surface, blended with a dedicated 'Upmix indicator' control element. This allows user interaction to place a visual representation of said immersive output formats on the 2D surface and move the associated soundobject positions to match the immersive output format.

Its sourcecode and prebuilt binaries are made publicly available to enable interested users to experiment, extend and create own adaptations.

Use what is provided here at your own risk!
